ArchBishop Benson Idahosa

The First African Evangelical Philantropist

Here is a timeline of Arch Bishop Benson Idahosa:

  • 1938 - Born in Benin City, Nigeria
  • 1946 - He began his education at the age of 8 in Salvation Army School in Benin City due to the financial status of his parents
  • 1952 - He attend Methodist school in Owo, Ondo state.
  • 1958 - Due to his passion for Education he went on to acquire his Diploma Certificate in Business Administration in London Benneth College, while he worked at the Bata Shoe Company as a sales man
  • 1961 - Through the help of his community head he was able to host his first crusade at Ajagbodudu, in Sapele
  • 1962 - For his desire to excel more and Gods Favour upon his life he was promoted in that Bata Shoe Company to a Supervisory position which was part of Gods plan and by that time he launched into ministry. He also enrolled as a correspondent student of a Bible College at Illorin. That same year, he held a crusade at Abraka.
  • 1968 - He inugrated his church "Church of God Mission International (CGMI)". Also, All Nations For Christ Bible Institute International (ANFCBII) was founded by Archbishop Benson Idahosa.
  • 1969 - He got married to Margaret Ekhoe, which later became Margaret Idahosa succeeded with 4 children (a boy and 3 girls)
  • 1971 - He earned a diploma in divinity from Christ for the Nations Institute in Dallas, Texas.
  • 1981 - He also acquired a Doctorate of Divinity from the Word of Faith College, New Orleans
  • 1984 - He also got a certification as a Doctor of Laws degree from Oral Roberts University
  • 1985 - He established the Redemption Television Ministry with a potential viewing audience of 50 million people.
  • 1998 - He died to go rest in the bossom of the Lord at the age of 60.
  • 2002 - Due to the impacts he has done in his nation Nigeria, The Federal Government of Nigeria founded Benson Idahosa University in forever memory of him
